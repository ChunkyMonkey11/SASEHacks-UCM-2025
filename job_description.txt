About the Job

Under supervision, the Fleet Analyst promotes customer-oriented services, responds to customer and vendor inquiries related to rentals, vehicle maintenance, billing, services, incidents, or concerns in a timely manner, and follows up to ensure satisfactory resolution. Oversees billing for Fleet Services, vehicle long/short-term rentals, insurance costs, fuel charges, vehicle repairs and maintenance, and other applicable services. Collaborates with several campus departments on vehicle accident/incident reports and driver safety programs to ensure compliance with applicable campus and UC policies and governmental regulations. Performs audits and reconciliation of various Fleet Services systems to ensure accuracy and compliance; makes adjustments or corrections as necessary. Provides other operational and administrative support to Fleet Services as needed. This position requires a valid California driver's license and enrollment in the DMV pull program to successfully perform the duties outlined in the job. The employee will be responsible for delivering UC fleet carts/vehicles to vendors for service or estimates as needed.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

- Assists in the analysis of the acquisition and use of vehicles.
- Compiles data and provides reports.
- Calculates the use of the daily rental pool by category.
- Calculates operating cost per vehicle, e.g., direct parts expenditures, overhead costs, etc.
- Retrieves and analyzes reports from systems to determine operating costs per mile.
- Reviews, analyzes, and summarizes data; creates reports; identifies trends; communicates trends, data, and operating costs to Fleet Manager and Financial Analyst.
- Manages the gas credit card program to include monitoring renewal process, card issuance, retrieval, and inventory.
- Develops fuel reports, analyses, and comparisons for the Fleet Manager and campus customers, as requested.
- Compiles Voyager fueling systems reports to determine trends in gas prices at specific fueling sites, and utilization of fuel by various vehicle types.
- Compiles required information related to fuel consumption, vehicle emissions, and other environmental regulations.
- Promotes customer-oriented services to ensure that performance standards, service guarantees and initiatives are delivered.
- Responds to customer inquiries regarding maintenance, emergency services, billing, etc. on a timely basis.
- Coordinates campus-wide automotive insurance claims and monitors repair expenditures and reconciles insurance payments.
- Maintains database for all campus-owned vehicles.
- Manages the coordination and installation of GPS devices, monitors activities, and maintains records and system data.
- Coordinates maintenance and repair of departmental fleet vehicles and equipment.

Fleet Analyst Position at UC Merced

The Fleet Analyst will be responsible for analyzing and managing the university's fleet of vehicles. This position requires strong analytical skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work with various software systems.

Key Responsibilities:
- Monitor and analyze fleet operations data
- Track vehicle maintenance schedules and costs
- Manage inventory of fleet vehicles and equipment
- Generate reports on fleet performance and utilization
- Coordinate with maintenance providers and vendors
- Ensure compliance with university policies and regulations

Required Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Business, Transportation, or related field
- Experience with fleet management systems and GPS tracking
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
- Strong database management skills
- Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities
- Critical thinking and problem-solving skills
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team
- Time management and organizational skills

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience in higher education or public sector
- Knowledge of vehicle maintenance and repair
- Familiarity with fuel systems and cost analysis
- Experience with administrative procedures and documentation
- Active listening and collaboration skills
